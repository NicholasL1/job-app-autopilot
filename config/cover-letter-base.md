# Cover Letter Base Template

<!--
  HOW THIS FILE WORKS:
  This is the base template that the /draft-cover-letter shortcut uses.

  Fields marked {{like_this}} are filled in automatically by Claude
  based on the job listing it reads in the current tab.

  Fields marked [like_this] come from your profile.yaml or resume.md
  and should already reflect your actual info.

  You do NOT edit this file per-application.
  Claude reads the job listing, reads your profile, and fills it in.

  After Claude drafts the letter, you review and adjust before using it.

  CUSTOMIZATION:
  - Edit the opening, closing, and structural paragraphs to match YOUR voice.
  - The clearer this template is about how you sound, the less editing you'll do.
  - Keep it under 350 words — hiring managers skim. Shorter is better.
-->

---

[Your Full Name]

[linkedin.com/in/yourprofile]

Hiring Team

Dear {{hiring_manager_name_or_"Hiring Team"}},

<!--
  OPENING PARAGRAPH (~2–3 sentences):
  Hook + position + one sharp reason you're excited about THIS company.
  Claude will pull {{company_specific_detail}} from the listing or quick research.
  Avoid generic openers like "I am writing to express my interest in..."
-->

I came across the {{role_title}} role at {{company_name}} and it caught my attention immediately — {{company_specific_detail_or_mission_alignment}}. With [X years] of experience in {{primary_skill_area}}, I'm excited by the opportunity to {{specific_impact_from_job_description}}.

<!--
  BODY PARAGRAPH 1 (~3–4 sentences):
  Your strongest, most relevant accomplishment for THIS specific role.
  Claude will choose from your resume highlights based on the job requirements.
  Lead with a result, then explain how you got there.
-->

At [Previous Company], I {{relevant_accomplishment_from_resume}}. This experience taught me {{transferable_skill_or_lesson}}, which directly maps to {{specific_requirement_from_listing}}.

<!--
  BODY PARAGRAPH 2 (~2–3 sentences):
  A secondary strength or different dimension (collaboration, scope, domain).
  This paragraph differentiates you from candidates with similar technical skills.
-->

Beyond the technical side, {{secondary_strength_or_soft_skill_with_example}}. I've found that {{brief_philosophy_or_approach_relevant_to_role}}.

<!--
  CLOSING PARAGRAPH (~2 sentences):
  Forward-looking, confident, not desperate.
  Express genuine interest in a conversation, not a plea.
-->

I'd love the chance to talk more about how my background in {{skill_or_domain}} could contribute to {{company_name}}'s work on {{product_or_mission}}. Thank you for your time — I look forward to hearing from you.

Warmly,

[Your Full Name]
